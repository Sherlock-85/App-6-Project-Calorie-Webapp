Title: Calorie Calculator
Description: An app that gets the user's weight, height,
age, city, and country, and scrapes the temperature of the user's location,
and calculates how many calories the user needs.
Objects: Calorie:
            weight(float)
            height(float)
            age(int)
            temperature(float)
            calculate()
          Temperature:
            country(string)
            city(string)
            get()
